How does it work

Simply place your ingredients under the base of the dicer, then pump the upper part until they are completely diced. Afterward, remove the upper part from the base for easy cleaning. The blade is also removable for hassle-free maintenance. Once everything is clean, reassemble it, and you're ready to Pump N Dice!
